Employment Dataset

This dataset presents a comprehensive view of employment statistics across various countries. Each record includes details such as the country, the individual's gender, their level of education (categorised according to the ISCED standard), marital status, the year of the data, and the employee count in thousands. The data spans from 2013 to 2023 and encompasses 154 unique countries.
